Primitiv / Harmoni

A two-mark system generated from a single equilateral triangle and the golden ratio. Primitiv is the primitive form; Harmoni is the harmonic series it generates. One colour, pure geometry, fully reproducible.

Two symbols, one geometric language.

Both marks share the same equilateral triangle and the same physical gap width, so they read as siblings. Primitiv expresses a linear golden-ratio scale; Harmoni expresses a concentric one — a single form generating an inner form.

02 — Shared Geometry

One equilateral triangle. Everything derives from it.

CanvasviewBox="0 0 100 100"
Apex (A)50, 10
Bottom-left (B)11, 77.5
Bottom-right (C)89, 77.5
Base width78 units
Altitude (height)67.5 units
Centroid (O)50, 55
Inradius (O→edge)22.5 units
Golden ratio φ1.618…

The two marks are tied together by a single rule: the negative-space gap is the same physical width in both. That width is set by Primitiv and matched by Harmoni.

Primitiv gap (between bands) = 2 × 0.0375 × altitude = 0.075 × 67.5 = 5.06 units Harmoni gap (between rings) = inradius × Δscale = 22.5 × 0.225 = 5.06 units ← matched

03 — Primitiv · construction

A golden-ratio scale, leaning into mass.

Primitiv mark3 bands · φ
Construction3 bands parallel to the right edge AC, converging at the bottom-left vertex B.
Band thicknesses1 : φ : φ²  (1 : 1.618 : 2.618)
DirectionSteps ascend left→right into a heavy mass at the bottom-right.
Gap0.0375 of the edge span each side → 5.06u perpendicular.
Fill3 solid polygons, #000, transparent gaps.

04 — Harmoni · construction

The same form, generating an inner form.

Harmoni marknested · even-odd
ConstructionConcentric triangles scaled about the centroid, filled even-odd.
Scales1.0 → 0.55 → 0.325
ResultBold outer frame · 5.06u gap · small solid inner triangle (one harmonic step).
GapΔscale 0.225 = 5.06u — identical to Primitiv.
FillSingle path, fill-rule="evenodd", #000.

08 — Usage

Keep it simple — the geometry does the work.

Colour

Black #000 — default White #fff — on dark
  • One colour only. Black on light, white on dark.
  • Negative space is transparent — never fill it.
  • No gradients, outlines, shadows, or rotation.

Clear space & size

  • Clear space ≥ the inner-triangle height of the Harmoni mark on all sides.
  • Minimum mark size ≈ 16px — both survive the favicon test.
  • Don't recolour, stretch, or re-letter-space the wordmarks (they're outlined for this reason).
  • Don't pair the Primitiv mark with the Harmoni wordmark or vice-versa.
